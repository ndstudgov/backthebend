Unity Gardens

Hosted by Unity Gardens

Location: 3701 Prast Blvd. South Bend, IN 46628

Number of Volunteers Needed: 100

Unity Gardens, a dignified free food model includes 40+ open gardens, free classes & summer wellness programs. By growing food, we grow food security! The mission is to improve community health physically, socially, environmentally & spiritually. See more: www.theunitygardens.org Help us grow!

The Unity Gardens' Back the Bend volunteer day hosted between 10-3 pm with flexible hours, helps prepare the LaSalle Square Unity Garden for active planting. Volunteers will help prepare raised beds, mulch pathways and other outdoor gardening activities. Of course, the chickens and goats will also be eager for attention. Join us on this 7acre urban ecosystem at 3701 Prast Blvd. South Bend, IN 46628. Please wear clothes that can withstand dirt and cold temperatures (mittens or gloves, hats, coats, etc). Closed toed shoes are a must!

Information about Unity Gardens uniquely dignified free food model can be found at www.theunitygaedens.org . We look forward to seeing you as we can host 100+ volunteers! Contact info- text or call 574-315-4361.
