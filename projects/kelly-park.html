Kelly Park

Hosted by Northeast Neighborhood and South Bend Venues, Parks, and Arts

Location: Kelly Park, South Bend IN 46617

Number of Volunteers Needed: 15

Kelly Park has been a 5 year long collaborative between the neighborhood members and the City of South Bend in improving this great community park. The neighborhood children started the process by receiving a small grant, and through donations, sponsorships, and City support the park has been completely reimagined. You can assist in preparing the park for the Arbor Day event that is going to be held April 28th. There is painting, dirt removal, and trash pickup.
