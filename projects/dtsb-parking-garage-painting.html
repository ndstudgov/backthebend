DTSB Parking Garage Painting

Hosted by Downtown South Bend

Location: 217 S. Michigan St

Number of Volunteers Needed: 10

DTSB is a non-profit organization in downtown South Bend that through its tireless efforts is helping raise awareness of all of the fun possibilities that are going on for all ages. Students will help touch up the yellow caution paint in the three parking garages managed by Downtown South Bend, adding to the safety and security of our downtown garages.
