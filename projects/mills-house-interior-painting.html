Mills House Interior Painting

Hosted by Indiana Landmarks

Location: 607 Leland Street, South Bend, Indiana

Number of Volunteers Needed: 12

Complete the interior painting of the walls and trim of the historic Mills House as it is readied for sale to a new homeowner. The project will save Indiana Landmarks $7,500 of cost and assist the new homeowner as they prepare to move into the house in May. Please do not wear open-toed shoes.
