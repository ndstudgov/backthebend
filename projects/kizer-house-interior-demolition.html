Kizer House Interior Demolition

Hosted by Indiana Landmarks

Location: 801 W. Washington, South Bend, IN 46601

Number of Volunteers Needed: 30

Join in completing interior demolition at the historic Kizer House in South Bend. The work will involve removing drywall and plaster, walls, old flooring and bathrooms on the interior of the house as Indiana Landmarks readies the site for further restoration. The work will involve lifting, use of prybars and hammers, carrying. The project will help save $20,000 of cost that Indiana Landmarks would have to spend reading the site for community use, office and housing. Please wear long pants and closed-toed shoes.
