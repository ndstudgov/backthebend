Help Repair a Neighbor's Home

Hosted by South Bend Code Enforcement, Rebuilding Together, and the South Bend Heritage Foundation

Location: 735 E Broadway St, South Bend, IN 46601

Number of Volunteers Needed: 25

Help repair the home of Mrs. Duke, whose house was recently struck by an uninsured drunk driver. Mrs. Duke is a caregiver for patients with dementia. She cannot afford the cost of repairs to her home and banks will not loan her the necessary money. Volunteers will work to repair the foundation and exterior paint.
